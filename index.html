Our Story

A Passion Project
Built on Love

When husband-and-wife team Bob and Colleen Coppola dreamed up Rosé Boutique & Wine Bar, they imagined a place where their two passions could live under one roof — Bob's deep love of wine and artisan food, and Colleen's eye for fashion and beautiful things.

Nestled in the Loveland Station complex in Historic Downtown Loveland, Rosé opened its doors in 2024 and quickly became the neighborhood's go-to gathering spot. Bob personally handcrafts every charcuterie board to pair perfectly with the wines he's curated from vineyards around the world, while Colleen hand-selects each piece of clothing, jewelry, and giftware in the boutique.

Whether you're here for a relaxed wine flight after work, a girls' night browsing the boutique with a glass of rosé in hand, or a weekend date night with a beautiful board and a bottle — this is your place. Welcome to Rosé.

— Bob & Colleen Coppola
